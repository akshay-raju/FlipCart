OnePlus 6T: Specification

Launched in October last year, OnePlus 6T price in India starts from Rs.37,999, which is for 6 GB RAM / 128 GB internal storage variant. Its other versions i.e. 8 GB RAM/ 128 GB internal storage is Rs. 41,999 and Rs. 45,999 for 8 GB RAM/ 256 GB internal storage option. Apart from these, OnePlus 6T mobile is available in two beautiful colours such as Mirror Black and Midnight Black.
