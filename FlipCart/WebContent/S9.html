Samsung Galaxy S9: Specification

Samsung Galaxy S9 is rumored to feature a 5.80-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1440 pixels by 2960 pixels at a PPI of 568 pixels per inch. Samsung phones are known to stand out for their camera quality. And the Samsung Galaxy S9 is expected to boast of a 12-megapixel primary camera on the rear and an 8-megapixel front shooter for selfies.
